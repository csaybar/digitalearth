2 Question N° 02

What is VV and VH stands for?

Both refer to the orientation of the microwave radiation transmitted by the radar. VV means single co-polarization, vertical transmit and vertical receive and VH means dual-band cross-polarization, vertical transmit/horizontal receive (See section 2.3.1 Veloso et al. 2017).
