2 Question N° 02

Summarize in a short paragraph the main elements of the ESA Sentinel-2 Level-2A specifications and how they are processed.

ESA Sentinel-2 Level-2A in comparison to Level-1C presents two principal improvements. The Atmospheric Correction which converts values from TOA to BOA (based on the LIBRADTRAN radiative transfer model) and adds an additional band called Scene Classification (SC). SC is a pixel classification map that is useful, for instance, to mask cloud and cloud shadow values.

What is “Sen2Cor”?

In a nutshell, Sen2Cor is a software maintained by ESA that permit generate Sentinel-2 Level 2A products from Level 1C products.
